Sign Language Alphabet Detector with Voice

The Sign Language Alphabet Detector with Voice is an AI-based system designed to recognize American Sign Language (ASL) alphabets in real-time using a webcam. The system translates hand gestures into text and speaks the recognized letter aloud, providing an interactive experience for users who may be deaf or hard of hearing. This project aims to bridge communication gaps and assist in learning sign language.
